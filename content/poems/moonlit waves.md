---
title: "Moonlit Waves"
date: "2023-03-18"
tags:
- "evergreens"
---

I count the bodies  
around me:  
one, two, three,  
four, five, six,  
seven.  

Seven bodies  
dipped in this  
almost unmoving  
waters  
lit only  
by the moon.  

I wait for any sound,  
any word.  

But all I can hear  
is the sound  
of waves approaching  
from afar.
