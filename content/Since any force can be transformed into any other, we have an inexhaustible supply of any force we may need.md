## Since any force can be transformed into any other, we have an inexhaustible supply of any force we may need # 

Every force in the Universe is capable of being transformed into any other kind of force by using suitableÂ means. There is thus an inexhaustible supply of any particular kind of force that we may need. Heat may be transformed into light and power by using it to drive dynamos. The vibrations of the air may be used to kill men by so ordering them in speech as to inflame war-like passions. The hallucinations connected with the mysterious energies of sex result in the perpetuation of the species.
