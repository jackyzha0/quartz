## Strong social support groups may sometimes only be about homogeneity, conformity, and xenophobia # 

[[Having social support is shown to lower the stress-response in primates]] [[Having social support is shown to lower the stress-response in humans|and humans]]. Also, [[the low social capital that comes with income inequality can lead to more stress and poor health]]. So having a strong network of friends and being in a community teeming with [[social capital]] can help with managing stress, but there is a potential downside. Amid all that nice, utopian [[social capital]] business lurks the inconvenient fact that a tightly cohesive, cooperative community with shared values may only really be about homogeneity, [[conformity]], and [[xenophobia]].
