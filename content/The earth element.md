## The earth element  #â³ 

The earth element is one of the main four of [[the great elements]]. It represents the qualities of solidity, density, and attractive forces. Any matter where attractive forces are in prominence are called earth elements. The internal earth elements include head-hairs, body-hairs, nails, teeth, skin, flesh, sinews, bones, bone-marrow, kidneys, heart, liver, diaphragm, spleen, lungs, intestines, mesentery, stomach, feces, etc.
