    dailies, as you can see here. The differences between capture-today and the
    template is a bit odd.

```elisp

      '(("d" "daily" plain
         "* Agenda\n** Tasks\n- [ ] %?\n\n* Notes\n** Ideas\n- \n\n* Journal\n** Morning\n- \n\n** Evening\n- \n"
```
