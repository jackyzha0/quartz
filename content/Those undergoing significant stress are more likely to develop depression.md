## Those undergoing significant stress are more likely to develop depression  #

People who are undergoing a lot of life [[stressor]]s are more likely than average to succumb to a major [[depression]], and people sunk in their first major [[depression]] are more likely than average to have undergone recent and significant stress. Laboratory studies also link stress and the symptoms of [[depression]]. Stress a lab rat, and it becomes [[ Anhedonia|anhedonic]].
